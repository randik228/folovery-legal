We do not share your data with:

4. Data security

  • In transit: all requests use HTTPS/TLS.
  • On device: authentication tokens are stored in Encrypted Shared Preferences using Android Keystore.
  • Pair isolation: Row-Level Security at the database level ensures one pair's data is technically inaccessible to another.
  • At rest: Supabase database encrypts data at the disk level.

5. Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained as long as you use the Application and have not requested deletion. Server backups are kept for up to 30 days and overwritten automatically.

You can delete your account and all related data:
